Privacy Policy

Scoundrel is a single-player card game that runs entirely on your device.

Information Collection

Scoundrel uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics, such as session count and basic device information. This data is used solely to understand how the game is used and to improve gameplay.

Personal Data

Scoundrel does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. There are no user accounts, logins, or social features.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies in accordance with Google's privacy policy. You can learn more here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children’s Privacy

Scoundrel does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if the game changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing.
