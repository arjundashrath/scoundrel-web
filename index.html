Survive the dungeon

How to Play

You begin with 20 health. Each room reveals 4 cards. You must resolve 3.

Monsters deal damage equal to their strength.

Weapons reduce damage, but once a weapon kills a monster, it can only kill monsters of equal or lower strength.

Health potions restore health, but you may drink only one per room.

You may avoid a room, but never twice in a row.

If your health reaches 0, you lose. If you clear the dungeon, remaining health is your score.
