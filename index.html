Scoundrel

Survive the dungeon

How to Play

You begin with 20 health. Each room reveals 4 cards; resolve 3.

Monsters deal damage equal to their strength.

Weapons reduce damage, but once used, can only defeat monsters of equal or lower strength.

Health potions: only one per room.

You may avoid a room, but never twice in a row.

Reach 0 health and you lose. Clear the dungeon to win.
